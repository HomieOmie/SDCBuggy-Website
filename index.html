                        RoleS

                        • PuSher

                          Responsible for getting the buggy around the uphill portions of the course, pushers, are fundamental to the sport of buggy. At SDC, we welcome all pushers whether you're looking to just hvae some casual fun pushing our buggies or take competition to the next level!

                        • Driver

                          Responsible for manuevering the buggy, the sport of buggy could not exist without Drivers. As easy as it may sound, drivers must follow their very specific race line for the most optimal rollout; small changes in the raceline can result in significant time differences, sometimes even seconds! At SDC, we do the best we can to give you a great driving experience while fostering safety.

                        • MeChaniC

                          Responsible for building new buggies and maintaining our current ones, Mechanics are very involved with the sport. At SDC, we foster a developmental environment where our mechanics bring their skillsets to the team to help us thrive, and we ensure that our mechanics walk out with hands-on experience like no other.
